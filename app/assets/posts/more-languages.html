Palabraset? Wortset? Motset? – Expanding beyond English

Wordset is expanding fast. We've got a lot of great people helping on projects, improving definitions, and adding example sentences. All of this is to help build the first open, collaborative English dictionary.

English isn’t the only language, though. We want to expand as soon as possible into other languages, but unfortunately Hampton and I only speak one.

That's why we’re looking for volunteers to help us expand into new target languages (TLs). Here's some of the ways in which you could help:

  • help choose an open-sourced starting set of words in the TL
  • help us understand the TL and any extra features that may need to be included in the UI/API
  • translate Wordset's buttons into the TL
  • foster a community around the TL by tweeting and encouraging edits

If you can help, please fill out a form explaining which language(s) you could help out with. We've listed out the top 20 languages according to Wikipedia, but would be open to other languages if the interest and community is there.

Note that as well as the target language, you would have to be familiar with English in order to communicate with Hampton and me (but I assume that if you're reading this, that won't be too much of a problem).

Looking forward to seeing which languages we'll tackle next!
